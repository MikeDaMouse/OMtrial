DANVILLE PARK

INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITY

152 Units | Value-Add | Decatur, AL (Huntsville)

Investment Summary

Standifer Capital Investment Opportunity

Standifer Capital is pleased to present an opportunity to invest in Danville Park, a 152-unit offering in the Decatur submarket of Huntsville, AL.

Key Highlights
Decatur-Huntsville, AL (Population ~ 775,000); 2.8% MSA unemployment rate (June 2022) Well-amenitized community with clubhouse, pool, dog park, fitness center, and green space
Class-B property in premier location with in-place cash flow 100% classic units allow for a clear path to renovation premiums
Off-Market Opportunity – Previous Buyer couldn’t perform. $1M Price Reduction Desirable unit mix with 50% 1-bedrooms and 50% 2-bedrooms, including 21% townhomes
Property is 97% leased; MSA vacancy of 2.2% (Q2 2022) Under market rents – 49% upside or ~$325 rent upside
Strong demographics of 73k Avg. HH Income Substantial sponsor investment (20%+)
Standifer owner 5 properties, 450 units and has been active for 5+ years in Huntsville Market 10-year, YoY, and forward 5-year rent growth of 5.2%, 16.7%, and 6.1%, respectively

Compelling Reasons to Invest in Danville Park

Mom & Pop Seller: The Seller is a partnership between five partners with minimal real estate experience. They have owned the property for 16 years and prioritized occupancy over rental increases. Existing Presence & Expertise: Standifer Capital has been active in the Huntsville market since 2017 and currently owns 5 properties totaling ~450 units. Standifer will leverage its existing infrastructure to efficiently oversee the asset and execute the business plan.
Well-Amenitized Property: Danville Park features top-of-market amenities, including a swimming pool, hot tub, fitness center with sauna, serene garden, and large clubhouse. Desirable Location: The Property is located off Beltline Road, the main thoroughfare in Decatur, and is within walking distance to several shopping, food, and entertainment destinations.
Attractive Fundamentals: The Decatur market has witnessed 16.7% YoY rent growth with a current vacancy of 2.2% and no units under construction or in the pipeline. Decatur is projected to have an average annual rent growth of 6.1% over the next 5 years. Substantial Discount: The Property was fully marketed and placed under contract for $15.75M before the buyer terminated the contract in July 2022. Due to Standifer’s relationships with key parties, it was able to secure the property off-market for a $1M haircut.
High Growth Market: Huntsville is ranked the #1 Best Place to Live by U.S. News. Several recent, large job announcements include Toyota, FBI, Space Command, and Meta.
Large Sponsor Co-Invest: Standifer Capital will be investing at least 20% of the total equity.

Business Plan Summary

Due Diligence
Standifer will conduct thorough diligence including physical inspections and lease audit.
Acquisition
Standifer will acquire the Property for $97k per unit (113k per unit all-in), which is well below replacement cost.
Repositioning
Standifer plans to reposition the Property over 2 years by solidifying this asset's value proposition for rents.
Operation
Standifer anticipates managing the asset to optimize cash flow.
Sale
Standifer anticipates selling the Property in the 5th Year of ownership after stabilizing the Property. A refinance is not currently contemplated.
